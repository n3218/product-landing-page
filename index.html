New Year #01

Description

Sweet Christmas composition with a symbol of the year in a wooden flowerpots, made of Danish spruce Nobilis. The composition is decorated in pink and gold colors. The composition includes piggy, glass balls, shinus, stipe, asparagus and decorative snow. With proper care, the composition will stay fresh 3-4 weeks.

Dimensions

Height: ........ about 25 cm

Diameter: ........ about 35 cm

Instruction

Add water to the center of the flowers composition once every 1-2 days as the floral sponge dries.
